Chicken Curry

This one's for all the chicken lovers out there. It is a traditional Kerala style chicken curry with roasted coconut masala. The piquant aroma and flavour of coconut adds a unique touch to this chicken recipe. This recipe best served with plain rice, roti, chapati or pulao.

Kacchi Biryani

Kacchi biryani is usually a featured dish for weddings and social gatherings and celebrations. Layers of meat, rice and potatoes are infused with warm and delectable blends of aromatic spices to prepare kacchi biryani. Whether it is a tender piece of mutton, potato, alubokhara(prune) or the rice itself, each spoonful is a mouthwatering surprise.

Hyderabadi Biryani

Hyderabadi biryani is a style of biryani from Hyderabad, India made with basmati rice and goat meat. Originating in the kitchens of the Nizam of Hyderabad, it combines elements of Hyderabadi and Mughlai cuisines.
